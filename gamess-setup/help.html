GAMESS Setup: help

How to get a password for downloading GAMESS source archive?

Your have to register your name and address at the GAMESS official website. Please choose 'GAMESS version ... for 64 bit (x86_64 compatible) under Linux with gnu compilers' at the registration.

Why my password is rejected?

The password for downloading GAMESS changes every week. Please get the latest password from the the GAMESS official website, if your password is older than one week.
